Contact Tracing

Contact Tracing is an important public health strategy to combat communicable diseases such as COVID-19. Due to recent surges of COVID-19 cases in Sri Lanka, the government has mandated logging their contact information prior to entering public places. However, the lack of respect many Sri Lankans have towards privacy and personal information is making the public sharing of personal information a huge privacy issue.

- Harassment
- Unsolicited marketing campaigns
- Identity theft

Paperless Contact Tracing is an entry-point to a paperless and secure solution to contact tracing by solving the above issues. It is a decentralized but shared contact tracing platform which can be used to collect and log personal information in public spaces.
Paperless offers a safe, seamless, and hassle-free process for both parties. Personal information logged through the Paperless Contact Tracing app will not be disclosed to anyone except for an authorized party with a legitimate request related to COVID-19.
