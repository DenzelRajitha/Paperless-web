01. What is Paperless?

We are an early-stage startup that aims to build a fully-fledged paperless-digitizing platform. It is an innovative solution to improve the productivity and efficiency of our day to day life, and also helps to reduce the global environmental impact of paper materials.
We believe that the world does not take full advantage of the capabilities of digitalization enough, in this digital age. Our aim is to provide sustainable, convenient, and most importantly, privacy-oriented digital solutions in the day to day life.

02. What is Paperless special?

Privacy
Manual handling of personal information is highly unsafe and could lead to many safety and privacy issues. Sharing contact details on pen and paper in public settings expose them to anyone, and it is impossible to guarantee that that information will not be misused. When contact is information is shared digitally, it is secure with limited access to who can access it. You will also have more control over which information you are willing to share if needed.

Convenience
Maintaining manual contact information records come with many practical issues. An organization that is supposed to maintain contact tracing information should allocate separate staff members to the task, allocate resources and space to store the information, and also allocate a considerable amount of time for the task. A digital solution such as Paperless increases efficiency, reduces the resources needed, and there will be no queues or wasting time at the entrance for both the staff and the customers.

No Paper clutter
Keeping the manual records and maintaining them will consume many papers, increasing your paper clutter and making a large negative impact on the environment. By using a Paperless contact tracing app, there will be zero use of papers and minimum impact for the environment.

03. What are the solutions you have?

We are still at an early stage, expediting our journey by providing a Contact Tracing Solution to address the current COVID situation first. While it is our current flagship feature, rest assured, we have many great features lined up to release in the future.

04. Contact Tracing in Paperless?

Contact Tracing is an important public health strategy to combat communicable diseases such as COVID-19. Due to recent surges of COVID-19 cases in Sri Lanka, the government has mandated logging their contact information prior to entering public places. However, the lack of respect many Sri Lankans have towards privacy and personal information is making the public sharing of personal information a huge privacy issue. There is a massive misuse of personal data shared for contact tracing at the moment, including harassment of women and unsolicited marketing campaigns mainly using phone numbers. Public exposure of personal information such as the NIC can also lead to more serious issues such as identity theft.
Paperless Contact Tracing is an entry-point to a paperless and secure solution to contact tracing by solving the above issues. It is a decentralized but shared contact tracing platform which can be used to collect and log personal information in public spaces.
Paperless offers a safe, seamless, and hassle-free process for both parties. Personal information logged through the Paperless Contact Tracing app will not be disclosed to anyone except for an authorized party with a legitimate request related to COVID-19.

05. Great! How can I join?

You can simply visit paperless.lk and register using your mobile number. You can also download our mobile app from Apple Store and Google Play. Just use your mobile number to Sign up and Sign in.

06. Okay. I have registered. Now what?

When you see a Paperless QR Code placed in locations you visit, just use your QR Code reader in the app to scan it. You will be asked to confirm your check-in. After confirmed, it will be logged securely.

07. Can scan the QR Code without installing your app?

Yes, you can. Just use your regular QR Code reader. It will open a dedicated web page for the location where you can securely log information. If you do not want to install our app, you can open a Paperless web account. Simply use your mobile number as the credential to log in from the web.

08. Nice! I need to get a QR Code to display at a location. How can I get it?

Visit paperless.incite.lk and log in using your mobile number. Click “Add your location” and fill the form. Once you submit the information, you will get the QR Code. You can simply print it and display it at your premises.

09. Can I show the same QR Code in many locations?

Please avoid using the same QR in multiple locations, since it will defeat the purpose. You can add as many locations as you want to your account, and get a unique QR code for each location.

10. You only ask for a phone number when we register. How can you successfully contact trace with that information

Every phone number is registered to a certain person, since the Government does not allow the use of unregistered phone numbers in Sri Lanka. It is an easy and secure way to collect contract tracing information. With the current manual methods, you cannot guarantee that everyone will be adding accurate personal information. Therefore, Paperless is not only more secure, but also more reliable.

11. What about people who don't use smartphones?

Unfortunately, we don’t have a solution for them at the moment. But, we are in the process of formulating a solution for that as well. Please bear with us for a few more days.

12. What specific information do you ask for when registering?

Right now, the only mandatory record is the mobile number. Additionally, users can update their address, NIC, and a few other personal information if they wish.

13. Can I, as a merchant/ owner, access the contact information of the visitors who arrive at my premises?

No. You cannot access any personal information unless users explicitly give their permission to share their personal details. Even with permission, we do not share sensitive information such as the NIC number with anyone.

14. How does a user give his consent to share his contact details?

It is an opt-in feature. Users can opt-in when they register by giving their consent.

16. How long do you keep our check-ins/location data on your system?

We do not keep your location data for more than two months. When the COVID-19 risk is no longer there, your personal data will be purged automatically.

17. How can we access our data?

Some of our engineering team has access to your data. None other than them.

18. How do you release information when an incident happens? Who can get them?

We only provide that information to proper authorities, only they request information for a public and verified incident.

19. Are you sharing information with the government?

Only when their request is related to a verified and public incident. Otherwise, we do not share personal information with any party, including the government.
